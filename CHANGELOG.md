# Changelog

All notable changes to the C³ standard are recorded here. Versioning follows [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org).

## v0.1.0 — 2026-05-30

Initial release.

- **Architecture** — three nested scopes (Creator · Content · Campaign); shared Pass/Partial/Fail chassis; relative vs absolute thresholds; veto-cap; cross-scope MECE boundaries; additive-within / multiplicative-across rollup with the Campaign Value Index `(ACE × ART × ROI)^(1/3)`; Creator × Content diagnosis matrix.
- **ACE** — Creator benchmark (Audience · Credibility · Engagement), 12 checks, 3 veto items (follower fraud, engagement fraud, brand safety).
- **ART** — Content benchmark (Appeal · Relevance · Transparency), 12 checks, 2 veto items (FTC disclosure, claim integrity).
- **ROI** — Campaign benchmark (Return · Orchestration · Impact), 12 checks, predict → measure → recalibrate loop.
